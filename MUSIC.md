

# MUSIC COMMANDS

**play**-Plays a song.

**search**-Searchs a song

**skip**-Skips a song

**stop**-Ptops the playing song.

**pause**-Pauses the playing song.

**resume**-Resumes the playing song.

**np**-Gives you playing song.

**queue**-Gives you queue song.

**volume**-Increase or decrease volume.
